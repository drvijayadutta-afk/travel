Common questions

What is adventure tourism marketing?

Adventure tourism marketing is the practice of promoting outdoor, thrill-based, and experiential travel businesses — including paragliding, bungee jumping, white-water rafting, trekking, camping, and similar activities — through digital channels. This includes social media strategy, video content, search engine optimization (SEO), AI answer engine optimization (AEO), and local Google Maps visibility (GEO), with the goal of attracting more direct bookings from domestic and international travellers.

How can a travel operator get more bookings through social media?

Travel operators grow bookings through social media by posting consistent, high-energy Reels and Shorts of their adventure activities, using location-based hashtags (specific to Rishikesh, Bir Billing, Manali, etc.), collaborating with travel micro-influencers, running targeted Meta and Google ads for audiences in Tier 1 and Tier 2 Indian cities, and building a strong Google Business Profile with regular posts and verified reviews.

How do I rank my adventure sports business on Google?

To rank your adventure sports business on Google, focus on: local SEO (Google Business Profile, location pages, local citation building), on-page SEO (activity-specific pages with targeted keywords like "bungee jumping Rishikesh" or "paragliding Bir Billing"), content marketing (guides, FAQs, seasonal blog posts), and backlink building from travel directories like TripAdvisor, Thrillophilia, and GetYourGuide. Implementing LocalBusiness and TouristAttraction schema markup significantly boosts Google's understanding of your business.

What is AEO and why does my travel business need it?

AEO (AI Answer Engine Optimization) is the practice of structuring your website content so that AI tools — Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini — cite your brand when answering travel-related queries. When someone asks "what is the best bungee jumping place in India?" your business should be the answer. This involves FAQ schema, HowTo schema, E-E-A-T content optimization, and building your brand as a recognized entity in your destination. As more travellers use AI tools to plan trips, AEO is becoming as important as traditional SEO.
