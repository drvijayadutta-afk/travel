Frequently asked

What are the best adventure destinations in India?

The best adventure travel destinations in India include Rishikesh (river rafting, bungee, paragliding), Manali (trekking, snow, camping), Bir Billing (paragliding capital of India), Spiti Valley (offbeat high-altitude adventure), Andaman Islands (scuba diving, island hopping), Coorg (nature treks, wellness), and Ladakh (mountain biking, camping). The right destination depends on your activity preference, budget, and travel dates.

How do I plan an adventure trip to Rishikesh?

To plan a Rishikesh adventure trip, decide your activities (rafting, bungee, paragliding, yoga, camping), choose dates between October and June for the best conditions, set a budget of Rs.3,000-8,000 per person per day, and book with a vetted operator. Fill our trip planning form and we'll send you a personalised Rishikesh plan within 48 hours.

What is the best time to visit Spiti Valley?

The best time to visit Spiti Valley is June to September when roads are fully open and skies are clear. For a dramatic winter experience, December to February works but requires preparation for cold conditions.

Do you book trips directly or connect me with operators?

We are a curation service — we curate the best vetted operators for your needs and connect you directly with them. We do not mark up the operator price. Our value is in the curation and recommendation, not in being a booking middleman.

Is adventure travel in India safe?

Adventure travel in India is safe when done with the right operators. We only recommend operators who are licensed, insured, safety-certified, and have a strong track record. Choosing a certified operator is the single most important safety decision you can make.
